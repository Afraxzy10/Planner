🏆 THE TOPPER RULE — PQ4R METHOD

For every chapter: Preview (2 min skim) → Question (write 3 questions you expect) → Read (full read) → Reflect (what's the main idea?) → Recite (close book, write key points from memory) → Review (recheck what you missed). This method doubles retention vs passive reading.

✅ DAILY NON-NEGOTIABLES

5:00–7:00am = Deep study (primary subject). Evening 5:30–8:00pm = Secondary subject + revision. Before sleep = write 5 key points from today from memory. Sunday = mock questions only, no new reading. Miss ONE day and you fall behind by 2 days.

🏆 THE TOPPER DIFFERENCE — What separates 1st rank from 3rd rank

Toppers don't read more — they recall more. After every study session, close the book and write what you remember. This active recall is 3x more effective than re-reading. The student who can reproduce content from memory in the exam hall is the one who tops. Practice this every single day.
